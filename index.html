          DESCRIPTION
        

        

          SCP-KΣ-0001, self-designated within the origin environment as
          ███████████████«kosstar the 1st», is an anomalous
          entity first documented in
          summer 201820██
          inside the social VR platform «VRChat.» The object cannot be
          classified as either a software bug or a user avatar; its behavior
          exhibits patterns
          incompatible with any known algorithm.
        

        

          The first reports came from a user (see Appendix K-1) who claimed to have «encountered perhaps the strangest shit in this game». The entity appears to not exist in the game’s account system, remaining invisible to the platform’s moderation systems. It did not respond to inquiries or physical contact, but reportedly,
          reacted to the mention of its designation.
        

        

          The object's appearance has not been consistently described by any
          observer. Witness accounts are contradictory and mutually exclusive —
          ranging from «a humanoid figure without a face» to «a patch where
          light itself breaks.» The Foundation believes the object
          has no fixed form within our perception████████████████
          and exists only insofar as it is perceived.
        

      


      
      

        

          SPECIAL CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES
        

        

          SCP-KΣ-0001 cannot be placed in any physical holding cell, as the
          object appears to
          possess no stable material form
          within our space-time continuum.
        

        
The following procedures have been established:

        
    
          
  • 
            Maintain monitoring of all keywords and the object's designation
            across all public VR/AR platforms and social networks.

          BREACH
        

        

          INCIDENT KΣ-0001-EXIT // RECORD PARTIALLY DELETED BY ORDER OF O5-█
        

        

          exact date withheld██.██.20██,
          in the settlement of
          undisclosed[CITY],
          several independent surveillance cameras recorded a brief spatial
          distortion matching the signature of VRChat anomalies. For
          approximately 47 seconds██ seconds
          local reality «glitched»: lights flickered outside the power grid,
          audio desynchronized, and the footage showed frames in which
          the object beyond the virtual environment[DATA EXPUNGED]
          was present.
        

        

          According to the recovered testimony of Agent █████ (see Appendix
          K-2), the object "did not exit through a screen. It exited through
          attention." Since then, SCP-KΣ-0001 is presumed to exist
          simultaneously in both environments.
          We do not know where it is «real.»
        

        

          The object's intentions remain
          unconfirmed. O5's working theory states: the object does not «want»
          anything in any human sense. It
          flows wherever attention
          arises. Attention is food. Attention is a door. Every time someone
          speaks its designation, the door
          cracks open, inviting it.
